The **Jackal Protocol** is a decentralized ecosystem for secure, user-owned data storage, powered by the **$JKL native token** and secured through the **Jackal Blockchain**.

## The Jackal Blockchain
The **Jackal Blockchain** is the backbone of the protocol. It provides secure, decentralized storage for data across distributed nodes.

The Jackal blockchain is secured through the PoS (Proof of Stake) consensus network. It is a Cosmos SDK chain that benefits both speed and interoperability of Cosmos-IBC network.

:::info
You can monitor the Jackal blockchain's metrics on the [MapofZones Jackal Dashboard](https://www.mapofzones.com/zones/jackal-1/overview).
:::

## The Jackal Storage Solution
**Jackal Storage** offers you a decentralized cloud storage solution for you to retain full ownership and control over your data.

Your data is distributed across multiple regions to maintain uptime and privacy even during regional outages or disruptions.

## Jackal Token ($JKL)
The **$JKL Token** is integral to the Jackal Protocol. his native token serves as the primary currency for transactions and staking within the network for crypto-economic security, governance, and access to storage and dApps.

You can use the JKL token to:

- Pay for Storage.
- Fuel dApps on the Jackal.
- Secure the Jackal network.
- Participate in Governance.
